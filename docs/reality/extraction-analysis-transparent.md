### **6. Value Created Estimate**

**Your claim**: "$80B value created"

**Methodology Needed**: What counts as "value created"?

**Possible approaches**:

**A) Productivity gains** (most rigorous):
- McKinsey estimates: AI could add $13T to global GDP by 2030
- 2025 fraction: ~$1-2T globally
- Assume 5% of global AI value is captured by current products: $50-100B

**B) Consumer surplus** (economic theory):
- Willingness to pay - actual price paid
- Example: ChatGPT Plus ($20/month) but users value at $100/month → $80/month surplus per user
- 10M paying users × $80 × 12 months = $9.6B (ChatGPT alone)
- Scale across all AI products: $50-100B plausible

**C) Revenue multiple** (weak):
- If AI companies had normal margins, revenue would be lower
- Monopoly rent = (actual revenue - competitive revenue)
- Hard to calculate without counterfactual

**Assessment**: Your "$80B value created" is **plausible but needs methodology**.

**Error bounds**: ±50% (wide range depending on definition)
